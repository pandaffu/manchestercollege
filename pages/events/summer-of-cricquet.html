An Unmissable Summer of Cricket in Manchester

Between now and September, Manchester will host 11 days of International cricket, as well as an exciting fixture list including the Vitality Blast and Specsavers County Championship.

Whether you’re a local cricket fan, or you’re coming to our city from further afield to catch a game, we look forward to welcoming you to what will truly be an unmissable summer of cricket in Manchester.
